FAQ
Common questions.
Do I own my website?
You own all of your content — your text, images, and branding. The subscription covers hosting and ongoing maintenance. If you ever cancel, we'll give you everything you need to move your site elsewhere.
What counts as a "reasonable edit"?
Things like updating hours, swapping out images, changing menu items, adding a new team member, or tweaking copy. Basically anything that isn't building a brand new page or feature from scratch.
How long does it take to build my site?
Most sites are ready within 2–3 weeks from when you provide your content. We'll walk you through exactly what we need before we start.
What if I want to cancel?
No long-term contracts. You can cancel anytime with 30 days notice. We'll help you transition your domain and any content so you're never left stranded.
Do you work with businesses outside of Moulton?
Absolutely. We're based in Moulton but work with clients anywhere. Everything is handled remotely — calls, emails, and a shared doc for content review.
What do I need to get started?
Just reach out. We'll send you a quick onboarding form to gather your logo, brand colors, content, and goals. From there we handle the rest.
