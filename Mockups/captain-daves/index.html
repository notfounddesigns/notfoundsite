About Us

A Moulton
tradition.

Captain Dave's has become a dependable stop in Moulton for seafood baskets, hearty Southern plates, and easy family dinners.

The atmosphere is casual, the food is familiar, and the service keeps locals coming back throughout the week.

  • 🦐 Fresh seafood and Southern comfort food, cooked to order every day they are open.
